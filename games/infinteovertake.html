Infinite Overtake

Support This Game

Overtake cars as long as you wish with your customized car on an endless road.

Keyboard Controls:
Space: Open/Close Menu
Arrow Keys or W, A, S, D: Throttle, brake, turn right/left
T: Change transmission type (Automatic/Manual/CVT)
G: Gear up, B or V: Gear down
M: Change driving mode (Eco/Trip/Sport/Race)
C: Change camera view
F: Full screen
